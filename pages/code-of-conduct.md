## IMOL 2027 Code of Conduct

IMOL 2027 is committed to a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone.

### Expectations

- Be respectful and considerate of others.
- Use inclusive language and respect personal boundaries.
- Participate in good faith and avoid disrupting sessions.
- If you see or experience concerning behavior, notify an organizer.

### Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, unwanted sexual attention, non-consensual recording or photography, sustained disruption, and violations of privacy are not tolerated.

### Consequences

Organizers may warn, remove, or ban participants, and may involve authorities when appropriate.

### Reporting

Contact: [imol2027@aalto.fi](mailto:imol2027@aalto.fi). Reports are handled confidentially and promptly.

By attending IMOL 2027 you agree to follow this Code of Conduct.
